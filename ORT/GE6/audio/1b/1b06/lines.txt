# One Wheel

"One wheel," said Chip.

"Look," said Chip.
"One wheel"

> "Look." said Mum. "I am on two wheels."

> "Look at me," said Kipper. "I am on three wheels.

> "Look at me." said Biff "I am on four wheels."

> "Look at Dad." said Mum. "He is on one wheel."

"Oh no," said Dad. "No wheels."
