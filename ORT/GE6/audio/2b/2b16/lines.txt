# Pond Dipping

Wilf and Wilma were at the pond.

The net got stuck.
>> "Help me pull," 
>> said Wilf.

They pulled the net.
It was stuck on some junk.

Wilma got a big stick
>> "Let's pull it out," 
>> she said.

Wilf and Vilma pulled.
They couldn't pull the junk out.

Mum and Dad helped
They pulled out an old pram.

>> Plop! 
>> A frog hopped out.
It made Wilf jump.

>> Splash! 
>> A fish jumped up.
"A pram full of fish," said Dad.

>> "Pram dipping!" 
>> said Wilma.
