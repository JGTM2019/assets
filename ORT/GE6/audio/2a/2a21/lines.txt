# Kipper's Balloon

Mum and Dad went shopping.

Kipper bought a balloon.

They went to the supermarket.
The balloon went bang.

Kipper bought a new balloon.
Dad went to the toilet.

Dad saw a balloon.
"Kipper's balloon!" he said.

Dad ran after it.

The balloon flew away.
Dad chased it.

The balloon was on a statue.
Dad got it down.

"Oh no!" said Dad.
