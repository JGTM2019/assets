# Adam Goes Shopping

Mum and Dad went shopping.

They took Adam and Yasmin.

Mum wanted a kettle.
Adam wanted to go home.

Yasmin wanted some shoes.
Adam was bored.

Dad wanted a track suit.
Adam was fed up.

Adam was tired.
He went into a tent.

Mum and Dad looked for Adam.
They couldn't find him.

Everyone looked for Adam.

Adam was fast asleep.
