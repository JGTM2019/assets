# The Water Fight (全录音未切割)

Everyone was hot.

The children wanted to go swimming.
Dad said "No!"

Biff qot the paddling pool.
Kipper filled it with water.

Chip pushed Biff in the water.
He grabbed the hose.

They had a water fight.

Mum got wet.
"Stop it!" said Dad.

Dad got a bucket of water.
He chased Chip.

Dad threw the water at Chip.
Oh no!

"Sorry!" said Dad.
