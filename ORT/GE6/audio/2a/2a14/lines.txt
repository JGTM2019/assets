# A Present for Mum

Dad had a little box.

It was a present for Mumn

Dad had an idea
He wanted to make Mum laugh.

Mum and Dad had a party

Mum gave Dad a present.
Dad gave Mum a big box.

Mum had a smaller box.
"What is it?" she asked.

The present got smaller...

...and smaller

...and smaller.

"This is for you," said Dad.

"And this is for you." said Mum.

>> "Oh Dad!" said Mum. 
>> "Oh Mum!" said Dad.
