# Lucky the Goat

Adam and Yasmin went on holiday.

They stayed with Grandmother.

Grandmother had a party.
Everyone wanted to come.

Grandmother had a kid goat.
The kid was called Lucky.

Yasmin looked after Lucky.

She gave her a bottle.

She gave her a cuddle.

She played with her every day.

The holiday was over.
Yasmin was sad.

Adam had spots.
They couldn't go on the aeroplane.

"Chicken pox,"said the doctor.
"Come back in two weeks."

>> "Lucky!"
>> said Yasmin.
