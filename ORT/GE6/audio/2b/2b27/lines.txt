# Pip at the Zoo

Pip went to the zoo.

Pip's dad was a zoo-keeper.
Pip helped her dad.

Pip gave the deer some food.

The deer pushed Pip over.

Pip gave the monkeys some bananas.
A monkey fook Pip's hat.

Pip gave the elephants some water.
The elephant took Pip's sweets.

>> Pip gave the parrots some fruit. 
>> A parrot pecked her finger.

"Do you want to feed the crocodiles?"

"No thanks," said Pip.
