# Creepy-crawly

Wilma had a creepy-crawly. She put it in the bath.

> Wilma called Dad. "Get it out," said Wilf.

"Ugh! I couldn't," said Dad.
Wilf called Mum.

"Get it out," said Wilf.
"Ugh! I couldn't," said Mum.

Wilma called Chip.
"Get it out," said Wilf.

"Ugh! I couldn't," said Chip
Wilf called Biff.

"Get it out," said Wilma
"Ugh! I couldn't," said Biff.

> Everyone called Kipper. "Get it out," said Wilf

"Easy!" said Kipper.
